How do I use this?
  1. Enter your details to create your signature.
  2. Add your company name and details.
  3. Add profile or company logo image and select position to display or not.
  4. Select font and design color.

How do I add signature into my email client?
To add your new email signature to your email client, simply click Copy to Clipbaord button and paste in to your email client (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, etc.) to do this use Ctrl + V on PC or command + V on Mac.
Why my email signature doesn't look as show on preview?
Don't worry, this can be fixed easily. You can try these solutions:

  1. Try using a modern web browser version to create your signature.
  2. Go to gmail.com and log in to your email account.
  3. Click the cog in the top right corner, and select 'settings' from the drop down menu.
  4. Add signture to your email client settings, get code of your signature and paste directly into some native email clients.
  5. for further queries you can send email us to Support.
